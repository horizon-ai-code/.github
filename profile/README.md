### About Us
Horizon AI is a technical collective dedicated to the democratization of autonomous systems. We bridge the gap between heavy-compute Large Language Models (LLMs) and the agile, cost-effective world of Small Language Models (SLMs). By engineering frameworks that prioritize efficiency without compromising reasoning capability, we enable sophisticated AI deployment on edge hardware and private infrastructure.

---

### Field of Operation
**Multi-Agent Orchestration (MAO) | Edge AI**

We specialize in designing communication protocols and state-management systems tuned for models under 10B parameters (e.g., Phi, Llama-3, Mistral). Our domain encompasses distributed AI systems, autonomous swarm intelligence, and quantized inference optimization.

---

### Our Mission
To engineer the foundational layer for decentralized, multi-agent ecosystems. We believe the future of AI lies in specialized, interconnected agents that are fast, local, and private. We provide the tooling necessary to transform static SLMs into dynamic, goal-oriented collaborators.
