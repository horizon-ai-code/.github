## About Us
Horizon AI is a technical collective dedicated to the democratization of autonomous systems. We bridge the gap between heavy-compute Large Language Models (LLMs) and the agile, cost-effective world of Small Language Models (SLMs). By engineering frameworks that prioritize efficiency without compromising reasoning capability, we enable sophisticated AI deployment on edge hardware and private infrastructure.

## Field of Operation
We operate at the intersection of Multi-Agent Orchestration (MAO) and Edge AI. 
* **Core Specialty:** Designing communication protocols and state-management systems specifically tuned for models under 10B parameters (e.g., Phi, Llama-3-8B, Mistral).
* **Domain:** Distributed AI systems, autonomous swarm intelligence, and quantized inference optimization.

## Our Mission
To engineer the foundational layer for decentralized, multi-agent ecosystems. We believe the future of AI lies in specialized, interconnected agents that are fast, local, and private. Our goal is to provide the tooling necessary to transform static SLMs into dynamic, goal-oriented collaborators.
